About Us

iTSikhwal Pvt Ltd is an innovative tech company focused on building custom applications, games, and websites using modern technologies such as Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and the Metaverse.

The company aims to enhance user experience and interactivity through its unique software products and solutions. Providing Software Development and Designing in technologies like Web, Apps, and Desktop software. Also technologies like AR/VR/MR Games/Apps for mobile as well in Unity 3D. Created over a dozen applications, games, and websites designed for busy professionals.
